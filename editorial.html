Editorial

'To live is to relate' said the very famous philosopher and spiritual leader, J. Krishnamurti.'

As an extension of the same, we are publishing our very first newsletter, 'The FleetPost'- to relate to our organization in a better way and contribute to it more effectively. This is our very first newsletter having a mix of formal and informal stories, events and news.

This time we have covered a couple of events from the last quarter-our beloved cricket tournament and the prestigious innovation award won by Fleetguard. We also have a TOC puzzle published and I am already eagerly

awaiting your answers for the same! Last but not the least, we have a communication from our Managing Director and the Chairman. I'm sure you all are looking forward to hear what they have to say. So go ahead, enjoy the news while sipping your fresh morning coffee or tea.

Appeal for contribution

With a desire to make this newsletter inclusive and enriching, we welcome your contribution in any way you feel important. Apart from that, any suggestions and comments will be valuable! Please feel free to write to fleetpost@fleetguard-filtrum.com for any and every input that you may have.

- Mukta Godbole
