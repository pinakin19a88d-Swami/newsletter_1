Editorial

It gives me immense pleasure to release the first edition of our company newsletter 'FLEET POST'. As the saying goes by a very famous philosopher and spiritual leader J. Krishnamurti, 'To live is to relate' - 'FLEET POST' is an initiative to relate to each and every one in our organization. I'm really excited and also eager to establish an interactive platform through this. The management would have a more frequent dialogue with everyone and would also like to hear from you on anything and everything that you may feel like sharing with us.

FLEET POST brings a mix of formal and informal stories, events and news. This time we have covered a couple of events from the last quarter - the prestigious innovation award won by Fleetguard and the exciting cricket tournament. We also have a TOC puzzle published and would have regular TOC stories which would add fun while learning TOC. We would like to know your thoughts on the puzzle.

Through FLEETPOST, our Managing Director and the Executive Chairman would share their views and give us a first-hand update on the happenings.

With a desire to make this newsletter inclusive and enriching, we welcome your contribution in any way you feel important. Apart from that, any suggestions and comments will be valuable! Please feel free to write to fleetpost@fleetguard-filtrum.com for any and every input that you may have.

With Regards,
Sanjay Kulkarni
